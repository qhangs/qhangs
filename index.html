About Q+

Welcome to Q+! We’re an upbeat and welcoming hang-out group for queer people and allies based in Concord, CA. Our very first meetup was in September 2025.

Say hey and stay updated on our Discord here.

✨ What we do: We get together regularly for table games, walks, side quests, and chill hangs.

⚡ Vibe check: Our meetups are warm, welcoming, playful, and positive. That means you can show up on your own without knowing anyone in the group, and we’ll do our best to make you feel included and valued.
