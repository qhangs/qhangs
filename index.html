Concord Pride in the Plaza (Q+ Rendezvous)

📅 SAT, JUNE 6, 12:30-4p

📍 Todos Santos Plaza (2175 Willow Pass Rd, Concord, CA 94520)

Pride in the Plaza is happening at Todos Santos Plaza in Concord on Saturday, June 6 from 12 to 4 pm!

If you’d like to meet up with us while you’re there, we’ll be at the Rainbow Community Center booth at 12:30 pm. The Rainbow booth will be behind the main stage near the corner of Grant and Salvio.

We’ll hang there for 10-15 minutes and then keep rolling. If you miss us, just ping one of us in the chat and we’ll give you our whereabouts.

This gives us a chance to enjoy the event as a group, break off into smaller groups to explore, or just say hi and continue with your day!

If you haven’t been to Pride in the Plaza before, it’s ALWAYS a good time. Lots of great vendors, food trucks, and performances throughout the day. There’s always at least one drag queen, and rumor has it Funkmode’s various dance troupes will be performing again this year!

See you there 🤗

Accessibility: Todos Santos Plaza is fully wheelchair accessible. It will be noisy and likely draw 800-1,000 attendees.

Table games at the food court! 🌈🎲

📅 WED, JUNE 10, 6-8p

📍 Sunvalley Mall Food Court (1 Sun Valley Mall, Concord, CA 94520)

We’re back at the Sunvalley Mall food court for table games on Wednesday, June 10 at 6 pm!

If you haven’t joined us before, we play games that are easy enough to learn on the spot…with just enough moving parts to be interesting.

The games are just an excuse to hang out, chat, and laugh. So you definitely don’t need to be a “gamer” to have a good time.

Here’s how to find us: The food court entrance is on the east side of the Sunvalley Mall near the north end. We’ll be easy to spot in the large seating area just off to the side of the central space where all the food vendors are located. The hot tip is to park on the *freeway side* of the mall, right next to the food court entrance.

See you there!

Accessibility: The Sunvalley Mall food court is fully wheelchair accessible, with restrooms right nearby. It’s a little noisy, and there are plenty of scents from vendors. No cost to join or hang with us, and the food/drinks are optional. Lots of free parking, and easily reachable by local transit.

About Q+

Welcome to Q+! We’re an upbeat and welcoming hang-out group for queer people and allies based in Concord, CA. Our very first meetup was in September 2025.

Say hey and stay updated on our Discord here.

✨ What we do: We get together regularly for table games, walks, side quests, and chill hangs.

⚡ Vibe check: Our meetups are warm, welcoming, playful, and positive. That means you can show up on your own without knowing anyone in the group, and we’ll do our best to make you feel included and valued.
